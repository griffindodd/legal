This service is operated by Joel Griffin-Dodd ("we", "us"). We send daily sports predictions via SMS to opted-in recipients.

- To send our daily sports pick summaries

## What We Don't Do

- We do not sell, share, or trade your phone number with any third party

## Opting Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will be immediately removed and we will not contact you again.
